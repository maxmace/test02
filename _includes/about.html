Man-made music. Created for our shared cosmic adventure.

Fun, melody-driven space music. Rich evolving sound and orchestration to prevent ear fatigue.

deliberately designed to work great both as contemplative background music (ambient for study) or for active listening as a 1970s album oriented experience! SEE ALSO: Vangelis, Isao Tomita, Jean-Michel Jarre, Wendy Carlos, Brian Eno, Jonn Serrie, Tim Clark, Steven Halpern, Tangerine Dream, Klaus Schulze, Constance Demby, Michael Stearns

You may not have nostalgia for analog planetariums and 1970’s space music…. Yet.

The term “anemoia” describes a sense of longing for a time or experience that one has never lived through.

Imagine an era where--for the first time--the combined voice of the symphony orchestra and the new sonic palette of analog synthesizers combine to evoke the awe and wonder of cosmic grandeur.

Memory Wheel is your laser-fueled time machine back to an age where new sounds combined with images in space for the first time.
