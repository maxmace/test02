About

Man-made music.
Created for cosmic adventure.

Memory Wheel is a retro space music project that encourages thoughtful, contemplative fun. Focusing on rich electro/symphonic sound featuring dynamic, evolving orchestration, world class recording techniques and the best in audio fidelity.

These compositions are deliberately designed to work as both contemplative background (e.g. "ambient for study" 😄 ) or for active listening, like a 1970s album-oriented experience.

SEE ALSO: Vangelis, Isao Tomita, Jean-Michel Jarre, Wendy Carlos, Brian Eno, Jonn Serrie, Tim Clark, Steven Halpern, Tangerine Dream, Klaus Schulze, Constance Demby, Michael Stearns


You don't have nostalgia for analog planetariums and 1970’s space music….

Yet.

The term “anemoia” describes a sense of longing for a time or experience that one has never lived through.

Evoking the spirit of anemoia, imagine an era where--for the first time--the combined voice of the symphony orchestra and the new sonic palette of analog synthesizers combine to evoke the awe and wonder of cosmic grandeur.

Memory Wheel is your laser-fueled time machine to an age where projected light combined with sounds in space in a brand-new way.
