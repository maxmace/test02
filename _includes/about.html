Man-made music. Created for our shared cosmic adventure.

Melody-driven space music for encouraging thoughtful fun. Rich electro/symphonic sound featuring dynamic, evolving orchestration, world class recording techniques and the best in audio fidelity.

deliberately designed to work great both as contemplative background music (e.g. "ambient for study" :smiley: ) or for active listening as a 1970s album-oriented experience!

SEE ALSO: Vangelis, Isao Tomita, Jean-Michel Jarre, Wendy Carlos, Brian Eno, Jonn Serrie, Tim Clark, Steven Halpern, Tangerine Dream, Klaus Schulze, Constance Demby, Michael Stearns


You may not have nostalgia for analog planetariums and 1970’s space music…. Yet.

The term “anemoia” describes a sense of longing for a time or experience that one has never lived through.

Imagine an era where--for the first time--the combined voice of the symphony orchestra and the new sonic palette of analog synthesizers combine to evoke the awe and wonder of cosmic grandeur.

Memory Wheel is your laser-fueled time machine to an age where projected light combined with sounds in space in a brand-new way.
